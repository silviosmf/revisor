The LDA-based negative binomial regression model reveals that the surge in transaction volumes led to a deterioration in service quality, with this effect more pronounced for public banks. The findings highlight the importance of incorporating user feedback into application development to improve adoption and customer retention, ensuring appropriate service quality. This study contributes to the literature by providing a large-scale empirical analysis of m-banking service quality issues, offering actionable insights for banks to improve mobile banking experiences. © 2025 Elsevier Ltd"
